Welcome!

Wired Hoods is a social venture seeking to close the digital divide in Harlem and under-served neighborhoods across the U.S.

This local network provides free Wi-Fi to public spaces in and around The Martin Luther King Towers. For your convenience and interest, we will soon be posting a coverage map here as well as useful links to other neighborhood sites. 

We also plan to launch a "smart green" community website that not only promotes environmentally-sound living, but also celebrates Harlem, its unique culture and its rich heritage.
