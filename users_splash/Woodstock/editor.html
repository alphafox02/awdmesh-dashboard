Welcome

This is the home of Wireless Woodstock, a non-profit community service designed to give free, safe and measurable wifi internet access to the village of Woodstock, Vermont.

If you reached this site by hopping on the network while in town, you are now welcome to surf the web as you wish. If you reached us from anywhere else, feel free to click any of the pages to learn more about our community-driven initiative.

Here is a map of the approximate current wifi service area.

Wireless Woodstock was conceived and produced by the nice folk over at Haystack Digital and is owned by the Norman Williams Public Library.
